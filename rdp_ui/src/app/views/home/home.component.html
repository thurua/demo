Following the soft launch of the Open Electricity Market (OEM), households in Jurong with postal codes starting with 60, 61, 62, 63 and 64 now have the option to purchase electricity from a retailer of their choice. You, as a consumer, will now be given the power to choose! There will be no disruption to your electricity supply when you switch, the only difference will be what you pay.

Red Dot Power is proud to be one of the participating retailers in the OEM soft launch.
