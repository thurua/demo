Retailer of Last Resort Event (RoLR)
  1. A Retailer of Last Resort Event is an event upon which the Consumer’s retailer is unable to continue operations for various reasons. As a Retail Electricity Licensee, Red Dot Power will publish via its website that it is undergoing a RoLR event within one business day of its occurrence, the Consumer will also be informed via SMS or email.
  2. In the event of a RoLR event, Red Dot Power Consumers will be transferred to the MSSL on the default supply effective date unless the Consumer makes a customer transfer request before the default effective supply date.
  3. Upon the transfer of Consumers due to a RoLR event the MSSL will supply small Consumers at the regulated Tariff and large Consumers at wholesale electricity prices. Small Consumers are commercial/industrial Consumers with an average monthly consumption of no more than 2 MWh, or any residential Consumers.
  4. Consumers physical supply of electricity will not be affected during a RoLR event.

  1. During a RoLR event, the Consumer should be aware that Red Dot Power is required by the authority (EMA) to transfer the Consumers contact information (including but not limited to mailing address, electronic mailing address and telephone number) to the MSSL to facilitate the implementation of the RoLR Framework.

  1. Residential Consumers have Two (2) years from the issue date of the relevant bill to contest a dispute.

SP Group as both the MSSL and as the billing agent of Red Dot Power may also be required to attend such ADR Body as required.
